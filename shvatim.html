The Game

There are 4 mothers, and 12 children cards. The goal is to assign the children to each mother in the correct order.

The menu at the bottom allows you to do stuff to the cards - like shuffle, deal, stack, and fan in various ways.

To play, click on the mother you want to try playing. Then click on each of her children in order. When you think you have all the children in place, click on the 'finished' button.

This game was suggested to me by Rabbi Joshua Zuber in Boston.
